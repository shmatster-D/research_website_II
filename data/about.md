I'm a PhD student in the **Department of Physics** at Brown University, where I develop machine learning methods for predicting and analyzing wind farm flows. My research seeks to leverage data-based techniques to make reliable predictions for turbulent fluid systems with a focus on sustainability. I am currently advised by Prof. Brad Marston.

Before coming to Brown, I completed my Bachelors at the University of Massachusetts Amherst. My research during this time was advised by Prof. Varghese Mathai, where I analyzed nonlinear membrane responses to fluid-induced stresses and particle clustering in decaying turbulent flows.
